IMPORTANT AIMSE UPDATE

We are excited to share that in our new normal we will be hosting the AIMSE National Conference Virtually! We will kick things off with our Bootcamp on November 11th and then move forward with conference dates of November 16th, 18th and 20th. As always, AIMSE members will be provided with our valuable content and access to consultants and plan sponsors.

We at AIMSE are committed to maintaining the high caliber of content, education, access and sense of community that our colleagues have come to expect. Stay tuned as more details will be coming! We look forward to “seeing” you in November.
